Privacy Policy

Privacy
We take your privacy very seriously. Scrawl Keyboard does not connect to the Internet or store any data locally for later use.
What data do we collect ?
We do not collect anything that you do in our app.
* We do not connect to the Internet
* We do not track your location
* We do not access your photos
* We do not access your contacts book
* We do not have access to other keyboards on your device
* We do not log any keyboard activities
* We do not store any data locally
Why do we need full access for the Scrawl Keyboard ?
Full access is needed to copy your drawings into clipboard memory so you can paste them into keyboard text field. There is currently no other way to send custom images from third party keyboard extensions
